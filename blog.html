ARTICLE

An article tag specifies independent, self-contained content. An article should make sense on its own and it should be possible to distribute it independently f rom the rest of the site. Potential sources for the article element:

  • Forum post
  • Blog post
  • News story

Note:The article element does not render as anything special in a browser. However, you can use CSS to style the article element (see example on the right).
